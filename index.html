Pre Year-11

The Digital Technology course at Botany Downs Secondary College is a dynamic and forward-thinking program designed to equip students with essential skills and knowledge in the ever-evolving world of technology. This course recognizes the critical role technology plays in today's society and aims to prepare students for the digital challenges of the future. Students enrolled in the Digital Technology course engage in a wide range of topics, including computer programming, software development, and digital design. They learn to harness the power of coding languages, explore the intricacies of hardware and software systems, and develop the problem-solving abilities necessary for success in the tech industry. With a strong emphasis on hands-on learning and real-world applications, this course empowers students to become proficient in various digital tools and technologies. Whether aspiring to pursue a career in tech or simply wanting to become tech-savvy individuals, students in this program gain invaluable skills that are increasingly essential in our interconnected world.

DIP

The Digital Technologies with Programming (3DIP) course at Botany Downs Secondary College is an advanced program tailored for academically inclined students. Building upon the foundational computer programming skills acquired in 2DIP, this course delves deeper into the world of object-oriented programming with a focus on modularity and the development of graphical user interfaces. Additionally, students explore diverse areas of computer science such as complexity and tractability, computer vision, big data, computer graphics, formal languages, and network communication protocols. The curriculum equips students with the ability to apply complex techniques to design and develop computer networks. Notably, this course holds recognition from several prestigious New Zealand universities, including Auckland University, which offer prior learning (RPL) credits to students enrolling in computer science courses. NCEA Level 3 approved, this program prepares students for tertiary studies in computer science or careers in the IT and business sectors, providing a strong foundation for their future endeavors. A compulsory trip to a software company and the option of online courses enhance the learning experience, making this course a valuable stepping stone toward a successful future in the field of technology.
