This course is structured to reinforce and enhance advanced competencies in Digital Technologies, encompassing web and print mediums, a deep understanding of design principles, and the technological processes involved. Students will gain a diverse set of hands-on skills and will showcase their proficiency through the utilization of various multimedia and software applications required to complete project tasks. The software tools employed will encompass applications such as Photoshop, Illustrator, phpMyAdmin, Notepad++, or equivalent alternatives. In this course, students will be educated on and put into practice advanced tools and techniques in areas like HTML5/CSS3 for web development, as well as PHP and MySQL for database development. A particular emphasis will be placed on digital media and information systems, covering aspects such as web design, software integration with databases, and fostering an understanding of information systems.
